## Founding people & whenua

**Wayne Roberts** and **Taranaki whānau** — **six generations in agriculture** in Taranaki — anchor the company’s relationship to whenua, food systems, and long-horizon care for land and community.

This is **Māori development context** for how Coastal Alpine Tech approaches sovereign AI, agritech, and local-first systems: with kaitiakitanga and accountability, not extractive branding. Product features that touch iwi, whenua, or Māori data must follow Te Mana Raraunga and human/cultural review (see `COMPLIANCE.md`).

## Mission

Ship a **local-first NZ founder operating system** — skills-heavy agents under hard legal ceilings — so founders and EDAs can move faster without fake autonomy or offshore-default data practices.
