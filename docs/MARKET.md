## The market in one paragraph

New Zealand has **617,330 enterprises**; ~90% have fewer than 10 people. The tracked startup ecosystem (764-2,400 companies depending on definition) grew **+26.2% YoY** and attracted **$754m across 166 deals in 2025** - but only **47 new companies** got first funding, so founders are capital-efficient and time-poor by necessity. They run modern stacks (Next.js/TypeScript/Python + Xero) yet still drown in admin, GST, grants, RDTI evidence, and investor readiness. That admin burden is the product wedge.
