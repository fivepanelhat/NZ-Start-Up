## Short blurb (pitch / LinkedIn / one-pager)

Coastal Alpine Tech is a **Taranaki pre-seed** venture building **NZ Start-Up in a Box** - a local-first, human-in-the-loop agent fleet for New Zealand founders. We go to market with **Venture Taranaki** and regional programmes (including **PowerUp**-class acceleration), prioritise **paid pilots** over free trials, and treat **pre-seed VC** as a follow-on once pipeline and product proof exist. Global founder platforms such as **startups.com** support visibility and narrative; they do not replace NZ compliance depth, dual-licence IP, or relationships rooted in whenua and six-generation agricultural heritage in Taranaki.

## Medium blurb (deck / EDA intro)

**Coastal Alpine Tech** is pre-seed by design: R&D from **August 2025**, company founding **August 2026**, product first, capital second.

**Venture Taranaki** is our home-region economic development partner - the channel for founder enablement, accelerator pathways, operator intros, and eventual white-label cohort seats ("Nick, but scalable"). That is a better first mile than cold national VC.

**Pre-seed venture capital** in New Zealand remains scarce and thesis-driven. Our ask sequence is: (1) dogfood the fleet on our own company, (2) paid discovery and pilots with regional accounts, (3) EDA/accelerator packaging, (4) then angels and funds who understand sovereign AI, agritech, and NZ regulatory reality.

**startups.com** and similar global startup platforms are useful for **story, peers, and signal**. They amplify; they do not underwrite product-market fit in Taranaki. We use them carefully so the brand stays grounded in Aotearoa law, Te Mana Raraunga, and local customers.
