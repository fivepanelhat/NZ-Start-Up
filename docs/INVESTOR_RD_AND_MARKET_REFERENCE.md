## 3. Markets we are creating / serving - employment & economic benefit (Taranaki + NZ)

This section ties **addressable markets** (from Drive founder-intelligence and funding work) to **jobs and regional outcomes**. Figures from third-party market reports in founder docs are **cited as context**, not as CAT revenue forecasts.

### 3.1 Market wedges (from *Founder Intelligence Report*, May 2026)

| # | Market space | Why Taranaki / NZ | Employment / capability pathway |
|---|--------------|-------------------|----------------------------------|
| 1 | **Edge AI / TinyML** | Farms, rural plants, offline sites; Pi + Hailo field nodes | Technicians, edge ML engineers, hardware installers; local service firms |
| 2 | **Agentic AI architecture** | SME digital transformation without Auckland/US SaaS lock-in | NZ software engineers, agent designers, product managers |
| 3 | **Cross-platform AI apps** | Rural connectivity constraints; offline-capable clients | Full-stack / mobile engineers; regional digital agencies as implementers |
| 4 | **AgriTech AI + IoT** | Primary industries; dairy/hort/viticulture export economy | Agri-tech specialists, agronomists + data, co-op digital roles |
| 5 | **Agentic security** | Regulated NZ orgs; non-human identity explosion | Security consultants, red-teamers, compliance roles |
| 6 | **Sovereign / decentralised AI** | Privacy Act, Te Mana Raraunga, public sector procurement | Policy-literate engineers, iwi/tech partnership roles |
| 7 | **AI ethics & compliance** | Algorithm Charter, human oversight (HITL) | Governance, risk, cultural advisory (not pure "replace humans") |

**Design choice that protects jobs quality:** Product autonomy ceiling is **inform / draft / prepare / monitor / remind** - humans still **advise, sign, file, send, pay**. That is intentional for **UEM Act, tax, legal, and cultural** risk - and for **augmenting** founder and EDA capacity rather than fake full autonomy.

### 3.3 Capital & programme channels (from *Funding directory*, July 2026)

Non-dilutive and regional first (employment-friendly - builds capability without premature extraction):

- **RDTI** (IRD/MBIE) - tax credit on eligible R&D; requires logs this product supports 
- **Venture Taranaki** - ScaleUp grants, PowerUp Business Ready / Accelerator, Investment Month 
- **Regional Business Partner** vouchers via VT 
- **Sprout Agritech**, **AGMARDT** - agri innovation employment networks 
- Later: angels (Enterprise Angels, etc.), Icehouse, Blackbird NZ, Nuance, **NZGCP Aspire** matching 

**Suggested sequence (directory):** grants + accelerator (Aug 2026-Jan 2027) -> angels/pre-seed with pilot data (2027). Raising before pilots = worse valuation for same dilution.
