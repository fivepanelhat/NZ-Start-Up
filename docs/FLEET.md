# Fleet Roster

End-to-end NZ founder lifecycle as ~10 specialists. Autonomy column is the product.

## Core ten

| Skill directory | Digital employee | NZ integrations | Primary tier |
|-----------------|------------------|-----------------|--------------|
| `formation-officer` | Formation Officer | Companies Office, NZBN, IRD | Gold |
| `compliance-registrar` | Compliance Registrar | Companies Act, Privacy Act, WorkSafe, Employment NZ | Gold + Diamond |
| `grants-rdti-clerk` | Grants & RDTI Clerk | rdti.govt.nz, MBIE/EDA, AGMARDT | Gold + Platinum |
| `market-validator` | Market Validator | Stats NZ, web research | Gold |
| `gtm-pipeline-rep` | GTM / Pipeline Rep | CRM (drafts), NZBN firmographics | Gold |
| `content-comms-officer` | Content & Comms Officer | Scheduling APIs (pre-approved only) | Gold |
| `finance-clerk` | Finance Clerk | Xero/QBO read, bank feeds read-only | Gold + Diamond |
| `funding-analyst` | Funding Analyst | Investor research, cap table math | Gold |
| `legal-document-assistant` | Legal Document Assistant | NZ template outlines, Privacy Act checklists | Gold + Diamond |
| `board-chief-of-staff` | Board / Chief-of-Staff | All + memory | Platinum + Gold |

> Agents inform, draft, prepare, monitor, and remind.  
> Humans advise, sign, file, send, and pay.
