**For:** EDAs | accelerators | incubators | accounting firms 
**From:** Coastal Alpine Tech | Taranaki | built on Aether 
**Version:** product v0.8+

## Problem

NZ founders drown in formation, compliance, grants/RDTI logging, pipeline, and GST admin - while generic "AI employees" ignore NZ law and burn tokens on fake autonomy.

## Product

A **skills-heavy local fleet** that maps the real NZ founder lifecycle:

- Formation -> compliance -> RDTI/grants -> market -> GTM -> content -> finance -> capital prep -> legal drafts -> weekly board 
- **Autonomy ceiling is the product:** agents draft/prepare; humans file, send, pay 
- **White-label cohorts** for partners who want "Nick, but scalable"

## Why NZ-native

Companies Office / NZBN | IRD/GST prep | RDTI contemporaneous logs | regional EDA grants | Privacy Act | Te Mana Raraunga | UEM Act (no autonomous cold email)
