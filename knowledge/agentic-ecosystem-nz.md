## White-space statement (medium-high confidence, as of July 2026)

No public NZ player combines: **productised installable fleet + hard HITL + NZ jurisdiction depth (RDTI/GST/Privacy Act/UEM) + Te Mana Raraunga + local-first hybrid edge + dual licence + white-label EDA packaging**. The market is consultancies, big-tech agent deployments, one or two lighter SME tools, and academic proposals.
