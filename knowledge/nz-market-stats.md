**Anchor claim (high confidence):** ~90% of NZ enterprises have fewer than 10 people and cannot afford dedicated finance/ops/compliance headcount.

## Top five startup segments (2025-2026)

1. **Software & Data / SaaS** - largest by count; ~33% of large rounds; Xero-native, needs compliance + runway + RDTI logging.
2. **Fintech / Accounting / Payments** - Xero is the national standard; finance workflows are the wedge.
3. **Agritech / Foodtech / Primary** - 39+ foodtech startups tracked; core CAT strength (hybrid edge).
4. **Healthtech / Medtech** - ~15% of H2 2025 large rounds; high compliance burden -> HITL + Privacy Act value.
5. **Climate / Cleantech / Energy** - grant-heavy, RDTI-eligible, long cycles -> board pack + grants clerk value.

## Dominant founder tech stack

Frontend React/Next.js + TypeScript | backend Node.js or Python | PostgreSQL/Supabase | Stripe + **Xero** (overwhelming SME share) | Vercel/AWS | AI via OpenAI/Anthropic/Gemini APIs with rising local Ollama experiments. Founder-OS tooling remains fragmented (Notion + ChatGPT + Xero + spreadsheets).
