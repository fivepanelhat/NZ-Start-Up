# NZ Legal Boundaries (Product Design Summary)

**Not legal advice.** Confirm with an NZ lawyer before commercial shipping claims.

## Lawyers and Conveyancers Act 2006

- Unauthorised legal practice is an offence.
- Product may prepare **information and draft documents** clearly marked as not legal advice.
- Do not hold out as a lawyer or conveyancer.

## Financial Markets Conduct Act (FMC Act)

- Term sheets, SAFE comparison, investor materials are **preparation only**.
- Flag: “have your lawyer review” — financial advice regime may apply depending on context.

## Tax administration

- Preparing worksheets is OK; **filing and acting as tax agent** is not.
- Never move money or hold client funds.

## Unsolicited Electronic Messages Act 2007

- Autonomous cold email is a **legal risk**, not a UX preference.
- GTM skill: drafts only; human sends (and must comply with consent/unsubscribe rules).

## Companies Act 1993 / Companies Office

- Annual returns, records (s189-style obligations) → reminders and checklists.
- Incorporation and filings require founder (or authorised agent) authentication.

## Privacy Act 2020

- Applies if you host founder personal information.
- Local-first architecture reduces multi-tenant risk surface.

## Health and Safety at Work Act 2015

- H&S checklists for farms/worksites are educational.
- Does not replace PCBU duties or professional H&S advice.

## Human Rights Act (hiring later)

- Screening rubrics must not encode unlawful discrimination.
